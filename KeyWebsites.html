The following are some key websites with preparedness information that you may also want to monitor for changing conditions, guidelines, and local announcements:

Web Resources
  • Local Volunteer Opprotunites
  • FEMA's ready.gov website for businesses; includes continuity planning guidance
  • Community information, referral, and crisis line; will include disaster info. if an event occurs.
County Emergency Management WebPages

Your county emergency management office can be a valuable resource for emergency planning, risk assessment, coordination of emergency training/drills, and questions on public-sector emergency services and procedures.

Your County Health Department or Public Health Office
  • Chippewa County Health Department
  • Dunn County Health Department
  • Eau Claire City-County Health Department
Regional, State, and Federal COVID-19 Resources
  • Chippewa Valley COVID-19 Economic Recovery Task Force
  • West Central Wisconsin Regional Planning Commission (WCWRPC) COVID-19 Resource List
  • State of Wisconsin Re-Opening Guidelines for Places of Business
  • Wisconsin Department of Health COVID-19 Information & Resources
  • CDC COVID-19 Resources for Businesses & Employers
  • OSHA COVID-19 Resources and Guidance

The above websites have many additional weblinks to explore. Additional FAQs and suggested practices that may be helpful can also be found at the websites of health providers, trade associations, insurance agencies, attorneys, other public entities, etc. Our sincere best to you, your family, your employees, and your business during these challenging times.
