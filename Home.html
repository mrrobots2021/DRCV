Our Mission

If our businesses are prepared for disaster, then our communities are prepared. That's where we come in. Our mission is to help local buisinesses prepare for the worst by providing them instructional resources and guides on how to better prepare themselves tokeep their assets and employees safe in times of emergency. Furthermore, By ensuring our local buisinesses are prepared for when disaster strikes, we are able to help increase the resiliancy of our community as a whole.
